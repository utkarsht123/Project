BASIC IDEA

With increase in prices and the middle men costs we want to reduce that time and money consumed so a safe gateway for that is the base website and app that provides a clear contact for the patient to the pharmacy and the doctor with any restrictions , they can get easy access to the medicine that they need for the proper treatment This website can provide online prescriptions and medicine offered affordable, fast, and secure medical visits . They can send prescriptions directly to your pharmacy . Some websites also provide free, peer-reviewed information on prescription drugs and over-the-counter medicines

Working

The Features

1.It will have certified doctors on call to get online diagnosis for non emergency situations

2.It will work as an online pharmacy using the prescription the patient received from the same website.

3.Leftover non expired medicine that can be used at lower prices can be sold by the communal online store where the routines of medicine can be sold to those who need it and sold by those who have excess of it

4.The feature of voluntary location based search will provide the patient with the information about the cheapest sellers and the nearest access to that location.

Future development

Using fast drone based delivery will help the patient and the seller ,These can be connected to the website to further lower the cost of middle men and the delivery system The image recognition software can detect the seller of the excess medicine and will notify near by consumers about the amount and the cycle that the number of medicine will fulfil Also Database of voluntary patient information will be stored and be accessed by the patient and the doctor at their fingertips
